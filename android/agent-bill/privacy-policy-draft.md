# AgentBill Privacy Policy (Draft)

_Last updated: 2026-05-19. Publish at a public URL (suggestion: https://thumbgate.ai/agentbill/privacy) and paste that URL into Play Console > Privacy policy._

## Summary

AgentBill is a bring-your-own-key tool. We do not see your transcripts, your prompts, your audit results, or your AI API keys. Everything happens between your phone and the AI provider you chose.

## What stays on your device

- Your xAI / Anthropic / OpenAI API keys. Stored in Android DataStore. Never transmitted to any AgentBill server (we don't operate any).
- The transcripts you paste in for auditing. Sent only to the AI provider whose key you configured (xAI by default).
- The audit results returned to you.
- Your subscription state for the AgentBill Pro tier (see "Google Play Billing" below).

## What we never collect

- Transcripts, prompts, code, or any content you paste into the audit screen.
- Your AI provider API keys.
- Audit results or analytics derived from them.
- Personally identifying information (name, email, location, contacts).

## Third parties

- **xAI / Anthropic / OpenAI (whichever you configure):** Your transcripts are sent to the AI provider you chose, governed by their privacy policies. We do not proxy or store any of this traffic.
- **Google Play Billing:** If you subscribe to AgentBill Pro, Google handles the transaction per their billing policies. We receive an opaque purchase token to verify your Pro status. We do not receive your payment details.
- **Crash reporting:** Disabled in v0.1.0. If enabled in a future version, you'll be asked to opt in.

## Children's privacy

AgentBill is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Your rights

You can revoke API keys, delete the app to wipe all local data, or cancel your subscription in Google Play at any time. There is no AgentBill account to delete because there is no AgentBill account.
